Our Story

A Bar Born from Passion for Great Evenings

Zephyr Bar is Hiranandani, Oragadam's finest restro bar experience — where the spirit of Tamil Nadu meets world-class hospitality. Under the banner of King's Recreational Club, we have crafted a space where every detail is designed to delight.

From our handcrafted cocktail menu to our chef-curated cuisine, every evening at Zephyr is an experience in its own right. Whether you're here for a quiet dinner, a celebration, or a DJ night out — we're your place.

We have two dedicated entrances. The Main Entrance carries our Government TASMAC licence details and official registration plaques. A second side entrance is available for quick access to the dining hall and DJ dance floor.
